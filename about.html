About us

Family owned and operated,we perform all levels of building and remodeling. We offer start-to-finish solutions for every project. We also offer full design services.Quality Construction Guaranteed

Tim Brown has been in the construction business for over 30 years, 10 years in Pagosa Springs producing 50 local residential projects. As a long-time local builder Tim is very well respected and can supply first class references from past customers, bankers, lenders, architects, sub-contractors and friends. Tim oversees all phases of the construction process from the bids to the walk through. Plus, he will work closely with the architect to make sure of every detail. Tim operates his own equipment and won't hesitate to put on a tool belt or hop in his backhoe when necessary. He actually does much of the trim carpentry and excavation work himself!

Conscientious about construction costs Tim operates up-front with a Guaranteed Price. You will always know where your budget is and there is always enough money to finish the job right! Tim is very organized so there is virtually no downtime on the job sit, which means completion dates you, can count on. Tim's longtime presence in Pagosa has allowed him to develop strong relationships with his sub-contractors, many of whom have worked for him for years. This plays a vital role in the process of building your home on time and on budget! Tim takes great pride in his work and delivers quality workmanship time and time again. Every Tim Brown Construction homes comes with an excellent builder warranty giving you even further peace of mind! Tim will help you to understand the building process and plan for necessary decision well in advance. Building your new home should be a time of excitement, not stress - This is supposed to be fun and it can be! Tim believes in a commitment to community and continues to give back through local donation of over $50,000 throughout the past 3 years.
